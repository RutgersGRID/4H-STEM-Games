Initiatives for Change

The games industry is filled with passionate people working to create positive change. The organizations below are leading the charge in using games for education, environmental action, and social equity, helping to make the world a better, more inclusive, and more sustainable place.

Games for Change

A non-profit that empowers game creators and social innovators to drive real-world impact through games and immersive media. They address issues like education, human rights, and climate change.

Visit Website →

Playing for the Planet

Facilitated by the UN Environment Programme, this alliance encourages the video game industry to reduce its carbon footprint and integrate environmental themes ("green activations") into major games.

Visit Website →

Green Game Jam

An annual event run by Playing for the Planet where studios from around the world add environmentally-themed content to their games to raise awareness and funds for conservation efforts.

Visit Website →

AbleGamers

A non-profit charity dedicated to improving accessibility in the video game space. They work with developers and provide resources to ensure that people with disabilities can play and enjoy games without barriers.

Visit Website →

Gina Davis Institute

This research-based organization works within the media and entertainment industry to eliminate negative stereotypes and create gender balance, fostering inclusion and reducing stereotypes in media aimed at children.

Visit Website →

UNICEF RITEC Framework

The Responsible Innovation in Technology for Children (RITEC) project provides a framework to help developers create digital experiences that respect and support children's rights, focusing on safety, inclusivity, and empowerment.

Visit Website →

Minecraft Education

A game-based learning platform that uses the beloved world of Minecraft to teach a wide range of subjects, including coding, chemistry, history, and problem-solving in an immersive, collaborative environment.

Visit Website →

Roblox Education

Provides resources for students and educators to use the Roblox platform for learning. It brings concepts from physics, art, and coding to life in immersive 3D worlds that students already love to explore.

Visit Website →
